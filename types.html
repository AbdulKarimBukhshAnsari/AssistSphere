Types of Cancer

Understanding different types of cancer and their early warning signs

Breast Cancer

Common signs include lumps, changes in breast size/shape, and skin changes. Early detection through regular screening is crucial.

Lung Cancer

Watch for persistent cough, chest pain, and shortness of breath. Smoking cessation is key to prevention.

Colorectal Cancer

Changes in bowel habits, blood in stool, and unexplained weight loss are common symptoms.

Skin Cancer

Look for changes in moles, new growths, and spots that don't heal. Regular skin checks are important.

Prostate Cancer

Symptoms include difficulty urinating and lower back pain. Regular screening is recommended for men over 50.

Blood Cancer

Fatigue, frequent infections, and easy bruising are common signs. Includes leukemia, lymphoma, and myeloma.



Prevention Tips

Regular Screening

Schedule regular check-ups and cancer screenings as recommended by your healthcare provider.

Healthy Lifestyle

Maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and avoid tobacco products.

Sun Protection

Use sunscreen, wear protective clothing, and avoid excessive sun exposure.

Know Your Risk

Understand your family history and discuss risk factors with your doctor.
